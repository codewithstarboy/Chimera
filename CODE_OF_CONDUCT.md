# Code of Conduct

We are professionals. Keep your discussions strictly technical, engineering-focused, and signal-heavy.

**1. No Illegal Requests:** Do not ask how to attack systems without authorization. This is a low-level engineering and security research project. Asking for help to commit crimes will result in an immediate, permanent ban.

**2. No Script-Kiddie Behavior:** Do not open issues asking for basic Android troubleshooting, how to root your phone, or low-effort "how to hack" tutorials. Read the documentation, figure out your environment, or do not use this tool.

**3. Strict Professionalism:** Keep the signal-to-noise ratio high. We have zero tolerance for spam, toxicity, or drama. Disagree on code, architecture, and JNI bridges—no personal attacks.

Violations of this compact will result in an instant ban from the repository. Respect the codebase and the engineers maintaining it.
